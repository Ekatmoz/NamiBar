The Sushi Bar welcomes lovers of Japanese cuisine all year round, in winter and summer for the third year now.

  • We operate pick-up points in neighboring cities seasonally or throughout the year, and we also deliver our freshly made sushi to hotels and restaurants for sale.
  • We are constantly expanding our offer so that we can always serve you with new products!
  • During the winter season, it is already a tradition to hold sushi-making courses for hobby chefs.

Feel free to contact us for events and company parties.

Why Choose Sushi Bar?

Sushi is a popular Japanese dish that is made with vinegared rice and typically includes raw fish or seafood. Sushi is often considered a delicacy and is enjoyed by many for its fresh, light flavors.
